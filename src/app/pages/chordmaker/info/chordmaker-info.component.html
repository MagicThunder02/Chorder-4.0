Chordmaker Info What is Chordmaker? Chordmaker is a free chord encyclopedia where you can search for chords and their features by selecting thier notes. How to use it? SELECT You can select a note by clicking on it. The first note will be the tonic of your chord. C C E E G G FIND All the chords found with the combinations on notes selected will be displayed. Click on a chord to see all its traits and infos. CM
